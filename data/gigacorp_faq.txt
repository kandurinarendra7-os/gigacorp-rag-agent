[Section: Company Overview]
GigaCorp is a fictional online retailer of electronics and home goods, founded in 2015.
GigaCorp ships to over 40 countries worldwide from three regional warehouses: North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific.
Customer support is available via chat, email, and phone.

[Section: Shipping Policy]
Standard shipping within the United States takes 3-5 business days and costs $4.99, or is free on orders over $50.
Expedited shipping within the United States takes 1-2 business days and costs $14.99.
International shipping is available to over 40 countries. Delivery times range from 7-14 business days depending on destination.
Shipping to India specifically takes 8-12 business days and costs $19.99 for standard international shipping, or $39.99 for express (4-6 business days).
Shipping to Canada takes 5-8 business days and costs $12.99 standard.
Shipping to the United Kingdom and European Union takes 6-10 business days and costs $16.99 standard.
All international orders may be subject to customs fees and import duties, which are the responsibility of the customer.
Orders are processed within 1 business day before shipping, except on weekends and public holidays.
Tracking numbers are emailed to customers within 24 hours of shipment.

[Section: Return Process]
GigaCorp accepts returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging.
To initiate a return, customers must log into their GigaCorp account, go to "Order History," and select "Start a Return."
Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items. For all other returns, the customer pays return shipping costs.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after GigaCorp receives the returned item.
Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged.
Exchanges for a different size or color are processed the same way as returns, followed by a new order.
Digital products and gift cards are non-refundable.

[Section: Business Hours]
GigaCorp customer support is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern Time.
On Saturdays, support is available from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern Time.
GigaCorp customer support is closed on Sundays and major U.S. public holidays.
Live chat support is available 24/7 for Premium and Business tier customers only.
Warehouse and fulfillment operations run Monday through Saturday, excluding public holidays.

[Section: Service Tiers]
GigaCorp offers three membership tiers: Basic, Premium, and Business.
Basic tier is free and includes standard shipping rates and email-only support during business hours.
Premium tier costs $9.99/month and includes free standard shipping on all orders, 24/7 live chat support, and a 45-day return window instead of 30 days.
Business tier costs $29.99/month and includes free expedited shipping, a dedicated account manager, 24/7 phone and chat support, and a 60-day return window.
Premium and Business members also receive early access to sales events and exclusive discounts.
Members can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel their tier at any time from the account settings page; changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

[Section: Payment and Billing]
GigaCorp accepts all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.
Membership tier subscriptions are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time with no cancellation fee.
Sales tax is applied based on the shipping destination in accordance with local tax laws.
